The Sanctuary

Located in the heart of Sarasota, Lyla's parrot sanctuary is a haven for parrots of all shapes, sizes, and colors. The sanctuary provides a safe and nurturing environment for these beautiful birds. Lyla works tirelessly to rescue and rehabilitate parrots in need, providing them with a second chance at life.

About Lyla

Lyla is a passionate parrot lover with a heart as colorful as the birds she cares for. Born and raised in Sarasota, Florida, Lyla's lifelong fascination with these magnificent creatures led her on an extraordinary journey to establish her very own parrot sanctuary.

Our Mission

At Lyla's Parrot Sanctuary, the mission is simple: to promote the well-being and conservation of parrots through education, rehabilitation, and responsible ownership. We believe that every parrot deserves a loving and forever home, and we are committed to raising awareness about the challenges facing these magnificent birds in the wild.

Rescue

We do not offer adoption of our rescue parrots, we will however provide them with a new forever home at the sanctuary.

The Sanctuary

Lyla's parrot sanctuary is a safehaven for parrots. We rescue and rehabilitate parrots in need, providing them with a second chance at life.

About Lyla's Sanctuary

Lyla's parrot sanctuary is a small family and friends operation on the outskirts of Sarasota. We are located on a lush quiet peaceful acre. We are closed to the public, our aim is not to be a zoo but a calm home to these beautiful birds.

Our Mission

The mission is simple: foster the wellbeing and conservation of parrots who need a new home for whatever reason that may be. We believe that every parrot deserves a loving and forever home.

Rescue

We do not offer adoption of our rescue parrots, we will however provide them with a new forever home.
